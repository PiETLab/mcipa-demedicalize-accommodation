# Public Feedback Policy

We welcome your feedback on this project. Whether you have a general comment about the paper or would like to respond to a specific passage, you can use the [[Provide Feedback/Provide Paper Feedback|Provide Paper Feedback]]  or [[Provide Feedback/Provide Segment Feedback]] forms.

We have designed the forms so that you do **not** need a GitHub account to submit feedback, even though your feedback gets captured on our github repository.

Your submission will be published as a **public but anonymously-submitted GitHub issue** so that comments, questions, and suggestions can contribute to an open and transparent discussion. Others will be able to read your submission and any subsequent discussion.

Please do **not** include medical information, accommodation documentation, student numbers, confidential information, or other personal or identifying information that you would not want to appear publicly.

Providing your name is optional. If you choose to include a name or pseudonym, it will also be published publicly as part of your submission.

We are still developing our protocols to describe the types of responses contributors can expect, the timelines for responses, and how feedback contributors can be kept informed.  In the meantime, we have provided the 'View submitted feedback on github' button a way for you to see the Issue on the issue tracker, and you are welcome to note the url of the issue and to follow along.

Thank you for helping improve this work.
